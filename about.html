About QR Studio

A lightweight responsive QR code and UPI payment QR generator for GitHub Pages and modern browsers.

Back

What QR Studio can do

  • Generate QR codes for plain text, URLs with UTM fields, Wi-Fi, contacts with birthdate, email, phone/SMS, events, and UPI payments.
  • Scan QR codes using camera, upload, drag-and-drop, or pasted images.

FAQ

Does QR Studio work offline?

Yes. After first load, the app caches its core resources and local vendor libraries.

Can I create an event QR?

Yes. Use Event to encode a VCALENDAR event with title, description, location, start, and end time.
